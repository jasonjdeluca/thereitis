## Demo Narration Script

Welcome to There It Is, a live multiplayer bingo game for earnings calls.

Start by choosing a company. The game creates a session and gives you a 5x5 bingo card filled with short phrases connected to that company's earnings calls.

From there, share the room link with anyone watching along. Each player gets their own board, and everyone can mark phrases as they hear them during the call.

The card is built for mobile, so it is easy to keep open while listening. Tap a tile when a phrase comes up, watch the board fill in, and race toward bingo.

The goal is simple: make quarterly calls easier to follow, more social, and a little more fun.

There It Is is unofficial and unaffiliated with the companies it supports. It is not financial advice, not investment research, and not a recommendation to buy or sell anything.

## FAQ Content

**What is this?**
There It Is is a live multiplayer bingo game for earnings calls. Players get a 5x5 card of short company-specific phrases and mark tiles as those phrases come up.

**Is it official?**
No. There It Is is unofficial and unaffiliated with the companies it supports. Company names are used only to identify playable call themes.

**How do I get more companies added?**
Send a request through the feedback form. New companies are added after transcript source review, phrase generation, QA, and human approval.

**Is it free?**
The beta is intended to be free while the game, company coverage, and feedback loop are being tested.

**Is this financial advice?**
No. There It Is is for entertainment and social gameplay. It is not investment research, financial advice, or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security.
